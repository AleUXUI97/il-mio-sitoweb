I’m an Artist

Hello, I’m Alessandro, a UX/UI Designer based in Milan, Italy. My background is rooted in art. I studied at an Art School and later attended the Akademia Sztuk Pięknych in Gdańsk, further expanding my artistic perspective in an international context. I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Sculpture from the Brera Academy of Fine Arts in Milan, where I developed a strong sense of form, balance, and visual expression. Over time, I shifted my focus toward digital design, studying UX Design at Start2Impact University to create meaningful, user-centered experiences. I’m also passionate about traveling and exploring different cultures. These experiences continuously expand my perspective and help me design with greater empathy, clarity, and awareness of diverse user needs.
