I’m an Artist

I’m Alessandro, a UX/UI Designer based in Milan. I design digital experiences that aim to balance clarity, usability, and visual expression. My approach comes from a background in fine arts, where I studied sculpture and developed a strong understanding of form, composition, and visual structure. I focus on user-centered design, with attention to interaction logic, accessibility, and consistency across interfaces.

How I work

01

Research

I ask questions, listen, observe and collect insights to understand the real problem, before making assumptions and jumping into solutions.

02

Strategy

I turn messy information into a clear direction, because “just make it pretty” is not the best strategy.

03

Design

I create wireframes, visual systems and interfaces that feel coherent, useful and, why not, pretty and polished.

04

Prototype

I build interactive prototypes to test flows and interactions with real users, so they can destroy my assumptions.

05

Refine

I refine and fix what needs improvement and obsess over details nobody asked me to obsess over, until everything feels intentional.
