Hello there!
I’m Alessandro, a 28-year-old from Milan, Italy. I’ve always been passionate about art and I love working on creative projects that allow me to fully express myself.
I’ve dedicated my life to the arts: I studied at an Art School and later graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Sculpture from the Brera Academy of Fine Arts in Milan.
Over time, I decided to apply my skills and knowledge in a new direction, exploring different fields and challenging myself in new ways.
With this purpose in mind, I began studying UX/UI Design at Start2Impact University.
